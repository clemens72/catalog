I wanted to try my hand at arranging horns. So I thought about which horns I liked and decided to start with focusing on James Brown's arrangements. (Before I really started the grand endeavor of Michael's horns.)

Took my blues jam and doubled the bpm to create a generic James Brown backing track and voila!
